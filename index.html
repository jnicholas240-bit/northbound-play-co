About Jeffrey
JN
Jeffrey Nicholas
Dad · Anchorage Resident · Reluctant Weather Checker

I'm a dad of four boys living in Anchorage, Alaska. Briana and I moved here because Alaska is the kind of place that makes you feel like your kids are growing up inside something real. The mountains. The moose. The trails that make the Lower 48 look like a golf course.

I started keeping notes on trails after a few disasters — the ones where we drove 40 minutes, got to the trailhead, and immediately had a full crew meltdown before mile 1. This guide is those notes. Field tested over multiple seasons, with four different ages, across every weather condition Anchorage has thrown at us.

The hike is the context. The point is the conversations, the questions about tides, the moment your kid goes quiet and looks at the mountains. Alaska is the best place in the world to raise kids who love the outdoors. These trails are a good place to start.
